Covid 19
Covid 19 (Corona Virus) is a flu bacterial virus that originated out of China in 2019. The contagious virus has plagued most countries in the world as well has closed all public interaction (shops, transport, events). Symptoms of Covid 19 include cough, lack of oxygen (hard to breath), diahorea, cold and fever. Many can be asymptomatic as well.
